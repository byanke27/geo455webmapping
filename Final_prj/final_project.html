Map Layers and Controls

Location of Stadiums: Stadium markers show where each stadium is located in the U.S.

Search and Navigation: Use Stadium name search, the mini map, scale bar, and full-extent button to explore efficiently.

Proportional Circles (Spending): Circle size represents total football program spending for the selected year. Teams with a gold ring finished the season ranked in the AP Top 25.

Performance Layer (Win %): Circle size represents program spending, while fill color indicates win percentage for the selected year. Green = high win rate, red = low win rate.

Density Heat Map: Heat intensity is weighted by distance to other stadiums, showing the high density of Divsion I athletics on the eastern half of the U.S."

About the AP Poll

The Associated Press (AP) Poll ranks the top 25 college football teams at the end of each season based on votes from sportswriters and broadcasters nationwide. A gold ring on a circle indicates that team finished the season ranked in the AP Top 25, representing one of the best programs in the country that year.
